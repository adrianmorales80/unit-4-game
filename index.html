Crystal Collector

Instructions
  1. 1. You will be given a target score at the start of the game
  2. 2. There are four crystals to the right. By clicking the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  3. 3. You will win by matching your total score to the target number.
  4. 4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. 5. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
